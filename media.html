Media



We have a long track record of providing consultation, advisory, onscreen expert and research services for broadcast media productions. Notable commissions include:



  • Who Do You Think You Are (Australia, SBT) – consultation, research services, onscreen experts (series 1-11, 2005 to date)
  • Who Do You Think You Are (Ireland, RTE) – consultation, research services, onscreen experts (series 1 & 2, 2006-2009)
  • Who Do You Think You Are (UK, BBC) – consultation, research services, onscreen experts, web content, interactive content (series 1-4, 2004-2007)
  • Nick Knowles’ Original Features (UK, UKTV) – research services (Series 1-3, 2010-2014)

  • Missing Millions (UK, ITV1) – research services, onscreen experts (2011)
  • Find My Past (UK, Yesterday) – research services, onscreen experts (2011)
  • Tracing Your Roots (UK, BBC radio 4) – research, co-presenter (Series 1-5, 2007-2011)
  • Live the Dream: As Seen on Screen (UK, ITV3) – research services, co-presenter (2009)
  • Secrets from the Attic (UK, ITV1) – research, on screen experts (2008)
  • Not Forgotten (UK, BBC) – research services, onscreen experts (2007)
  • Empire’s Children (UK, Channel 4) – research services, web content (2007)
  • So You Think You’re Royal (UK, Sky One) – consultation, research services, co-presenter (2007)
  • Hidden House History (UK, History) – research services, co-presenter (2006)
  • History Mysteries (UK, BBC) – research, onscreen experts (2005)
    •


We regularly provide content for national newspapers, magazine and trade publications. Major commissions include researching and writing a weekly column for the Telegraph (2005-2008) and providing the editorial team for Your Family History magazine (2010-2013).

We also have a media team who can film and edit material for broadcast or web streaming. Our media partner, Family History Films, was started by the founders of two leading production companies, Paul Hurley and Jon Bell, who have a collective wealth of experience in creating and managing content for broadcasters, leading corporations and consumer brands.
