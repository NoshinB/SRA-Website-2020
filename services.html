Our Services


We’ve been working in archives, libraries and research institutions for over twenty years and cover a wide range of areas , although we’ve listed our four most popular areas below for you to explore – genealogy, house history, heritage and media (just click on the images below to find out more).

In addition, we’ve contributed to academic projects; researched rights of way and property boundaries; written corporate histories; and supported legal cases and police investigations.

We believe every commission is as unique as the research trail we follow, which is why we like to spend some time understanding your needs before any work is actually agreed – fill out our contact form and tell us about your project, and we’ll put together a research plan for discussion.
